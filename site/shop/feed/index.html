Amazing miracles occur in Africa every day. None is more amazing or moving than what Little Elsa experienced.

Read about her encounter with Jesus Christ and His message for her and for us. While reading her story, learn about the history of Ethiopia, one of the oldest countries on earth, life as a child in a rural African village, miraculous transformations to Christianity, and men who are doing God’s Great Commission work.

This book is part of Elsa’s effort to be obedient to Christ’s instructions to her to Tell The Whole World That I Am Real. By making a purchase for yourself or as a gift for someone who might benefit from its message, you are helping Elsa. All proceeds will go towards her continued education or to ACTS’ efforts to expand its ministry.
